# Distribution Policy

## Goals
- Keep the project usable for personal research and repair.
- Avoid distributing materials that may violate vendor terms, copyright, or anti-circumvention rules.

## Guidelines
- Prefer original code, redacted traces, and high-level behavioral notes.
- Do not publish firmware blobs, private keys, or sensitive captures.
- Review third-party materials before copying them into `third_party/`.

## Review Trigger
Update this policy before any public binary release, hardware kit release, or publication of vendor-protocol details.
